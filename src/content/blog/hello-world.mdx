Welcome.

This is where I'll write about things that matter to me — the craft of building software, the philosophy that informs how I think about systems, and the occasional thought that doesn't fit neatly into either category.

## Why Write?

Building software is an act of translation. You take something messy and human — a need, a frustration, an aspiration — and you render it into something precise. Writing is the same act, reversed. You take precise thoughts and let them become messy, human, shared.

I've always believed that the best developers are the ones who can think beyond code. Not because code doesn't matter — it does, deeply — but because the problems worth solving are rarely purely technical.

## What to Expect

- **Technical essays** on architecture, systems thinking, and the craft of building
- **Philosophical musings** on technology, consciousness, and the human experience
- **Project deep-dives** into things I'm building and why

This is a space for slow thinking in a fast world.
