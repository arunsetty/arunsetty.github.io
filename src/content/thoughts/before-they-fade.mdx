This site finally has a corner for the small things.

I write a lot. Usually short, often half-formed, sometimes only true on the day I write them. They've been living in notes apps, draft messages, half-finished pages. This is the place where I'll catch them before they fade.

Fair warning if you're reading this in the future: I'm not optimizing for permanence here. These are notes, not essays. Half of them will be wrong. The other half will eventually be wrong too.

Let's see how it goes.
